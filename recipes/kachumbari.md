# Kachumbari



*condiment, salad*


---

- *6* onion 
- *6* tomatoes
- *1/2 cup* lime juice
- long green chile pepper (optional)
- salt to taste and to brine onions

---

1. Slice tomatoes and onions paper thin.
2. Soak onions in salt and water.
3. Rinse brine off.
4. Seed and mince chiles.
4. Combine ingredients.
